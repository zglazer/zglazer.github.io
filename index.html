About Me

I am a graduate of UW-Madison, having studied both computer science and biochemistry. I have had a life long interest in computers and want to continue to do great work in the software industry.

I received my BS in biochemistry in December 2010 and soon after accepted a position at Epic Systems Corporation in Madison, WI. After 2 years of work in healthcare IT, I decided I wanted to further my education in computer sciences. In December 2013 I was awarded a certificate in computer sciences from UW-Madison with high honors.

I currently work as a Senior Clinical Informatics Analyst at Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach. My technical industry knowledge and custom development are assisting the organization to improve patient care.

In my free time I enjoy learning new programming frameworks and tools. Recently I have been working with Python and the Flask web microframework.

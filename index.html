About Me

I am a graduate of UW-Madison, having studied both computer science and biochemistry. I have had a life long interest in computers and want to continue to do great work in the software industry.

In healthcare IT I have worked for both a software vendor (Epic Systems Corporation) as well as a large hospital system. I continue to have interest in improving patient care using my technical knowledge.

My most recent project has been Gusto Sites, a small web development shop I started with my partner Jordan Walker, aimed at serving our local Chicago community.

In my free time I enjoy learning new programming frameworks and tools. Recently I have been experimenting with Go to enhance server administration.
